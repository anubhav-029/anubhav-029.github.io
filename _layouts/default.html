Anubhav Vishwakarma

Hello, I am a Robotics graduate student at University of Washington where I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Karen Leung. My research interest lies at the intersection of using both classical and learning based methods to develop trustworthy autonomous systems.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from Ramaih Institute of Technology, Bangalore, India. Here my most time went into designing and building efficient aircraft morphologies for various UAV applications.

Please feel free to explore my research work and projects below! and do reach out if you'd like to connect.
