Anubhav Vishwakarma

Hello, I am a Robotics graduate student at the University of Washington, where I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Karen Leung and work at the Robot Learning Lab directed by Dr. Byron Boots. My current research interest lies at the intersection of using both classical and learning-based methods to develop trustworthy autonomous systems.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bangalore, India. During my time there, I spent most of my time designing and building efficient aircraft morphologies for various UAV applications. After graduation, I shifted my focus towards building autonomous drones for indoor navigation in GPS-denied environments and SLAM.

Please feel free to explore my research work and projects below, and do reach out if you'd like to connect!
